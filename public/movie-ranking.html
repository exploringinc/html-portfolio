The Best Three Movies

Ridley Scotts's Top 3 Movies of all-time

Blade Runner (1982)

A masterpiece of science fiction, Blade Runner takes place in a dystopian future where human-like robots called replicants have been created to do dangerous or undesirable work.

Gladiator (2000)

One of Ridley Scott's most commercially successful films, Gladiator tells the story of a Roman general (played by Russell Crowe) who is betrayed and seeks revenge against the new emperor.

Alien (1979)

Ridley Scott's first major film, Alien is a classic of the horror/sci-fi genre. A crew of space travelers encounter a deadly alien creature on a remote planet, and the tension and scares are expertly crafted.
